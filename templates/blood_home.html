Donation Process

The donation process from the time you arrive center until the time you leave

1 - Registration

Begin by registering as a donor, providing your basic information and medical history to ensure eligibility

2 - Screening

Undergo a brief medical examination to assess your health and suitability for donation, including a check of vital signs and hemoglobin levels.

3 - Donation

Proceed to the donation area where trained staff will collect your blood through a sterile, safe process that typically takes around 10-15 minutes.

4 - Refreshment

After donation, replenish your fluids and enjoy a light snack to help your body recover, ensuring a positive donation experience and well-being
